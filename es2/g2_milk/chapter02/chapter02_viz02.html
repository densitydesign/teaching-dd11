Chapter 2 - Is drinking milk healthy?

By authors - Category

Introduction

Till now we examined the different positions about a specific topic and we introduced the typology of authors present in the controversy.

Here we are going to understand better how, how much and where they are speaking.

Protocol

Inside the queries - Protocol

The typology is defined thanks to the categories of websites found in the Wikipedia definition of “Website”, in the section “Types of websites”. This variety of typologies may be arbitrarily interpreted in any numbers of ways.

How to read it

The visualization shows the numbers of the articles per category; each square represents an article and each colors is an author of the articles.

Findings

The authors who are speaking in the majority of the websites are the experts and the writers. The first ones, more or less, discuss each theme at the same way, the second ones, instead, are focused more on intolerance and external agents.

The authors less present are the industries, who direct their attention to the external agents and nutrients.

On the other side, looking at the category, the more common one is the nutrients.
